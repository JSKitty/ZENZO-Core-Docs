\subsection{Detailed Description}
S\+H\+A-\/224, S\+H\+A-\/256, S\+H\+A-\/384 and S\+H\+A-\/512 interface.

S\+H\+A-\/256 has been published in F\+I\+PS 180-\/2, now amended with a change notice to include S\+H\+A-\/224 as well (which is a simple variation on S\+H\+A-\/256). S\+H\+A-\/384 and S\+H\+A-\/512 are also defined in F\+I\+PS 180-\/2. F\+I\+PS standards can be found at\+: \href{http://csrc.nist.gov/publications/fips/}{\texttt{ http\+://csrc.\+nist.\+gov/publications/fips/}}
