\subsection{Detailed Description}
W\+H\+I\+R\+L\+P\+O\+OL interface.

W\+H\+I\+R\+L\+P\+O\+OL knows three variants, dubbed \char`\"{}\+W\+H\+I\+R\+L\+P\+O\+O\+L-\/0\char`\"{} (original version, published in 2000, studied by N\+E\+S\+S\+IE), \char`\"{}\+W\+H\+I\+R\+L\+P\+O\+O\+L-\/1\char`\"{} (first revision, 2001, with a new S-\/box) and \char`\"{}\+W\+H\+I\+R\+L\+P\+O\+O\+L\char`\"{} (current version, 2003, with a new diffusion matrix, also described as \char`\"{}plain
